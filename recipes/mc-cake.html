Minecraft Cake

A delicious in-game treat, as seen in Minecraft.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Food coloring (green, blue, red)
  • Buttercream frosting
  • Edible decorations (Minecraft-themed if available)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a square baking pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then add the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk, and mix until well combined.
  6. Divide the cake batter into separate bowls and add food coloring to create green, blue, and red batter.
  7. Pour each colored batter into the square baking pan, creating a pixelated pattern like in Minecraft.
  8. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool completely before frosting it with buttercream frosting.
  10. Decorate the frosted cake with edible decorations, adding a touch of Minecraft-themed fun.
  11. Serve your delicious Minecraft cake and enjoy a tasty treat that brings the blocky world of Minecraft to life!
